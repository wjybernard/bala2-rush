Privacy Policy — Bala2 Rush

Effective: 20 July 2026 · Developer: wjyb (Bernard Wijaya)

Bala2 Rush is a casual game. This policy briefly explains how the app handles data.

Data we collect

We collect no personal data. Bala2 Rush does not ask for your name, email, location, contacts, or any sensitive permission. There is no account or login.

Data stored on your device

High scores, the player name you type on the scoreboard, settings (sound/music), and game progress are stored only locally on your device (via browser/localStorage). This data is never transmitted anywhere and we cannot access it. Uninstalling the app or clearing its data removes this information.

Ads, analytics, and third parties

This app shows no ads, uses no analytics or tracking, and shares no data with third parties.

Children

The game is safe for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.
